Privacy Policy

At AutoPublisher, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we request necessary access tokens from third-party platforms (like TikTok) strictly for the purpose of publishing your content. We do not collect unnecessary personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your authentication tokens and connected account data are used solely to fulfill your requests to upload and publish videos. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any external advertisers or third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to ensure that your access tokens and publishing schedules are kept secure within our environment.

4. User Control

You can revoke AutoPublisher's access to your social media accounts at any time through the settings of the respective platforms (e.g., TikTok Security settings).
