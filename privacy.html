Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

AutoPublisher ("we", "our", or "the App") is a desktop-based content management tool designed for gaming creators. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application to connect with TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize AutoPublisher using the TikTok Login Kit, we strictly access the following scopes requested during authentication:

  • Basic Profile Information (user.info.basic): Used solely to verify your identity and display your profile picture/username within the local application interface.
  • Video Publishing Capabilities (video.upload, video.publish): Used strictly to upload and publish your selected local video files from your desktop to your TikTok account.

2. How We Store Your Data (Local Storage Only)

AutoPublisher is a client-side desktop application. This is a core part of our privacy commitment:

  • All authentication tokens (OAuth tokens) are stored securely and locally on your personal computer.
  • We DO NOT maintain external databases, and we DO NOT transmit, store, or process your personal data, tokens, or video files on any third-party servers belonging to AutoPublisher.

3. Third-Party Services

AutoPublisher uses TikTok's Official API Services to function. By using our application to publish to TikTok, you are also agreeing to be bound by the TikTok Terms of Service and TikTok Privacy Policy. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any external advertisers or third parties.

4. Data Retention and Revocation

Because your data is stored locally on your machine, you have full control. You can delete the application and its local configuration files at any time to remove your data completely. Furthermore, you can revoke AutoPublisher's access to your social media accounts directly from your TikTok Security settings at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@louai-aldalati.github.io
